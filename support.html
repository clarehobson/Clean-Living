Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Recovery Pro subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile tab, scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

Push notifications aren't working — what do I do?

Go to your device Settings → Clean Living → Notifications and make sure notifications are enabled. Then open the app and go to Profile → enable notifications from within the app too.

I purchased Recovery Pro but the app still shows free — what do I do?

Try logging out and back in. If that doesn't work, contact us at support@cleanliving.app with your Apple ID email and we'll sort it out.

How do I report a member or abusive content?

Tap the ··· menu on any post in the community feed and select "Report this post". We review all reports within 24 hours.
