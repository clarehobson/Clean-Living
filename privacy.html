2. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the EU (Ireland). All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are hashed and cannot be read by anyone, including us.

3. Camera and Photo Library

Clean Living requests access to your camera and photo library for two purposes: to allow you to upload a profile photo so other members can see your face, and to allow you to photograph event flyers so the app can automatically extract event details using AI. We do not store raw photos — only the uploaded profile image and any extracted event information. You can decline camera access and still use all other features of the app.

3a. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we store a device token on our servers so we can send you notifications about new messages, connection requests, and other activity. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app. We do not use push notifications for marketing purposes.

3b. In-App Purchases

If you subscribe to Recovery Pro, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple and store this against your account to enable Pro features. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used — for example, how many people visit, which features are popular, and which countries our users are in. Google Analytics does not collect your name, email, journal entries, or any personal content. It uses anonymised data such as page views and session duration. You can learn more about how Google handles data at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only services that process your data are:

— Supabase (database hosting, authentication) — stores your account and profile data

— Google Analytics (anonymised usage statistics) — no personal data shared

— Cloudflare (website security and performance) — processes web traffic to protect the site

— GitHub Pages (website hosting) — serves the app files

— Stream (real-time messaging infrastructure) — processes direct messages between members. Messages are transmitted and stored via Stream's servers. Stream does not have access to your journal, profile data, or any other account information.

— Anthropic (AI processing) — when you use the Recovery Companion feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic processes these messages in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not send your email address, journal entries, or other account data to Anthropic — only the conversation messages you type in the Companion chat. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, or marketing cookies.

6. Your Social Links and Anonymity

Your anonymity is a core principle of Clean Living. Your social links (Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook) are completely hidden from all other members by default. They are only revealed when you mutually connect with someone, and you choose which specific links to share with each individual connection. You can disconnect at any time, which immediately hides your social links from that person. The other person is not notified when you disconnect.

7. Journal Privacy

Your journal entries are stored in the cloud so they sync across your devices. They are linked to your account and are not visible to any other user, not even the admin. We do not read, analyse, or share your journal content.

7a. Recovery Companion Privacy

The Recovery Companion is an optional AI chat feature. When you use it, your typed messages are sent to Anthropic (the maker of Claude AI) via our secure Cloudflare proxy to generate responses. These messages are not stored on our servers and are not linked to your account in any permanent way.

Your conversation history is stored only on your device using your browser's local storage. A brief personal profile summary (such as your preferred name or how long you have been clean) may also be saved locally to make future conversations feel more personal. Neither the conversation history nor the profile summary is uploaded to our servers or accessible to us.

If you clear your browser or app data, this information will be erased. You are never required to use Recovery Companion — it is entirely optional.

7b. Community Feed

Posts you share on the community feed are visible to all signed-in members of Clean Living. Do not include personal identifying information in community posts that you would not want shared publicly within the app. You can contact us to request removal of a specific post at any time.

If you report a post, we store a record of that report (including the post ID and the reason you selected) so we can review it and take action. This information is only visible to the admin and is never shared with the person who made the post.

If you block another member, we store a record of that block so their content can be hidden from your feed. We are also notified when a block occurs so we can review the blocked member's recent activity. Your identity as the person who blocked them is not shared with the blocked user. You can unblock anyone at any time from your account profile page.

8. Children and Young People

Clean Living is open to users of all ages. If you are under 18, you should have the consent of a parent or guardian to use this app. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

— Access your personal data — you can view your profile and journal within the app at any time

— Correct your data — you can edit your profile at any time

— Delete your data — you can delete your entire account through the app, which removes all your data permanently

— Object to processing — contact us if you have concerns about how your data is used

— Data portability — you can export your journal entries from the app

To exercise any of these rights, use the features within the app or contact us at [email protected].

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed. We do not keep backups of deleted accounts.

11. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, row-level security on the database, and secure authentication through Supabase. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Cookies

Clean Living uses minimal cookies required for the app to function (authentication session). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. Google Analytics uses its own cookies for anonymised usage statistics.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app. The date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last updated.
