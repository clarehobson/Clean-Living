🌿Clean Living

Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Clean Living is a recovery app for people in the 12 step fellowship. We know the trust it takes to use an app like this, so we keep what we collect to a minimum and we never sell your data. This page explains, in plain English, what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

Clean Living ("we", "us", "our") is operated by cleanliving.app. If you have any questions about your privacy, you can reach us any time at hello@cleanliving.app.

2. What we collect

Your account

When you sign up we collect your email address and a password (stored securely and never visible to us in plain text). These are used to create and protect your account.

Your profile

Information you choose to add: a first name or username, your country and region, your clean date, an optional profile photo, an optional status, and any social links you decide to share. You control all of this and can edit or remove it at any time.

Your recovery activity

Content you create in the app, such as journal entries, step work, community posts, mood check-ins, meeting logs, service commitments, your sponsorship tree, and the events you say you're attending. Your journal entries and step work are private and visible only to you. Community posts are visible to other signed-in members.

Messages

If you message another member, the content of those messages is processed and stored by our chat provider so it can be delivered and shown to you both.

Your phone number

If you choose to add a phone number to your profile, we use it to send you important account and service messages — including updates about the app, your account, and changes that affect your ability to use Clean Living. These are sent by SMS. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message; you may still receive essential service messages where the law allows.

How you use the app

We collect basic, app-level usage information (for example, that a feature was opened or a meeting search was run) to understand what's working and improve Clean Living. We do not track your precise location, and we do not build advertising profiles.

Device permissions

  • Notifications — if you allow them, we use a notification token to send you alerts about messages, connection requests, and activity. You can turn these off any time in your device settings.
  • Camera & photos — only used if you choose to add a profile photo or photograph an event flyer so the app can read its details. Photos are not stored beyond the image you choose to upload.

3. The Recovery Companion (AI feature)

Clean Living includes an optional AI companion (Noah). When you talk with it, the messages you send are passed to Anthropic (the maker of Claude AI), through our secure proxy, only so a reply can be generated.

Your conversation history is stored locally on your device — not on our servers. A short summary of details you share (such as your preferred name or clean time) may also be kept on your device to make future chats feel more personal. Using the AI companion is entirely optional.

The Recovery Companion is a supportive tool only. It is not a therapist, sponsor, or medical professional, and it does not provide crisis support. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a helpline straight away.

4. How we use your information

  • To provide and run the app — your account, profile, journal, community, messages, and meetings.
  • To personalise your experience, such as your clean time and reflections.
  • To send notifications you've opted into.
  • To keep the community safe and respond to reports of objectionable content.
  • To improve the app and fix problems.
  • To meet our legal obligations.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data, and we never have. We share information only with the trusted service providers that make the app work, and only as needed to provide the service:

  • Apple — handles all subscription payments. We never receive or store your card details.
  • Supabase — our database and account hosting.
  • Stream — powers direct messaging.
  • OneSignal — delivers push notifications.
  • Anthropic — generates Recovery Companion replies (message content only, during an active conversation).
  • Cloudflare — provides the secure proxy that routes those AI requests.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the safety of our members.

6. Subscriptions

Recovery Pro is an optional auto-renewing subscription billed through your Apple ID. All payment processing is handled by Apple — we do not see or store your payment information. You can manage or cancel any time in your Apple ID account settings.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently remove your profile, journal entries, connections, social events, and associated data. This cannot be undone.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit and access controls on our database. No method of storage or transmission is ever completely secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

9. Your choices and rights

  • Access & correction — view and edit your profile and content at any time in the app.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from your account settings.
  • Notifications — turn push notifications on or off in the app or your device settings.
  • Camera & photos — decline access and still use the rest of the app.
  • AI companion — ignore the feature entirely if you'd prefer to keep your conversations private.

If you'd like help exercising any of these, email us at hello@cleanliving.app.

10. Children

Clean Living is open to all ages, in keeping with the fellowship. If you are under 18, you confirm you have the consent of a parent or guardian to use the app. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will let you know through the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@cleanliving.app and we'll be glad to help.

Clean Living is an independent recovery tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Narcotics Anonymous or NA World Services.
